FAQ

  • Home
  • FAQs
What services do you offer?
We provide exterior hand wash, waxing, interior deep cleaning, ceramic coating, paint correction, engine bay detailing, and more.
How long does a full detailing service take?
Depending on the package and vehicle condition, most detailing services take between 2–6 hours to complete.
How often should I get my car detailed?
For best results, we recommend a full detailing every 3–6 months, with regular maintenance in between.
Do you offer ceramic coating?
Yes. Our professional-grade ceramic coating protects your vehicle’s paint for years, enhancing gloss and making cleaning easier.
Is detailing safe for new paint or wraps?
Absolutely. Our detailing techniques are safe for all paint types and vinyl wraps, and we tailor methods for sensitive finishes.
Do you offer mobile detailing services?
Yes, we offer mobile detailing at your home or office for select packages. Contact us to check availability in your area.
What is paint correction and do I need it?
Paint correction removes surface imperfections like swirl marks, scratches, and oxidation to restore your vehicle’s finish.
How long does ceramic coating last?
Our ceramic coatings can last 2–5 years depending on product tier, maintenance routine, and driving conditions.
Do you use safe cleaning products?
Yes, we use pH-balanced, biodegradable products that are safe for your vehicle, your health, and the environment.
Can you detail classic or luxury cars?
Absolutely. We specialize in high-end and classic vehicle detailing and use non-invasive methods to preserve the original finish.
Do you offer packages or custom services?
Yes, we offer flexible packages and à la carte services to fit your car’s needs and your budget.
How can I book a service with CyberGuard?
You can schedule online, call us directly, or visit our location. Our team will guide you through choosing the right service.
