Residential

Homeowners lead the clean energy shift with efficient rooftop solar and battery backup. Our systems ensure lower bills and greater energy control.

Commercial & Retail

Retail stores and offices save costs and boost property value with solar. Our solutions include EV charging and smart commercial lighting.

Industrial

Factories and warehouses rely on high-capacity solar for stable, affordable power. We integrate systems with generators and industrial batteries.

Education

Schools and universities lower costs and inspire with solar-powered campuses. We also support learning with educational displays and tools.

Agriculture

Farms use solar to power irrigation, processing, and storage. Our rugged systems support off-grid and remote agricultural operations.

Government

We help government buildings, public lighting, and community spaces run on clean energy. Our team supports tenders and large-scale projects.

Hospitality

Hotels and resorts reduce bills and boost their green image with solar. Our elegant systems power lighting, cooling, and more.
