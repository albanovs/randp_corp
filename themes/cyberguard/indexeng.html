LLC Registration in the Kyrgyz Republic for Foreign Citizens

Information Appendix

This appendix is intended for foreign citizens (non-residents) planning to register a company in the form of an LLC (Limited Liability Company) in the territory of the Kyrgyz Republic.

1. Who Can Register an LLC

  • be a founder of an LLC (100% ownership is permitted);
  • be both the founder and director of the LLC;
  • be a founder and appoint another person as director.
There are no citizenship restrictions for registering an LLC.

2. General Procedure Overview

1. Preparation of documents
2. State registration of the company
3. Tax registration
4. Company seal production
5. Opening a bank account
6. Obtaining licenses (if required)

Most procedures can be completed under a notarized power of attorney, without the personal presence of the foreign citizen.

3. Documents Required from a Foreign Citizen

Mandatory Documents

  • Passport of the foreign citizen;
  • Notarized translation of the passport into Russian;
  • Contact details (phone, e-mail);
  • Notarized power of attorney for a representative (LLC registration, tax registration, obtaining a PIN, licensing, representation before state authorities).

Additional Information

  • A visa is not required for LLC registration;
  • Personal Identification Number (PIN):
    • if available ‚Äî the number is provided;
    • if absent ‚Äî it can be obtained by us under a power of attorney.

4. Information to Be Determined in Advance

  • full and abbreviated company name;
  • types of activities;
  • whether licenses or permits are required;
  • amount of charter capital;
  • executive body (director / general director);
  • legal address;
  • tax regime.

5. State Registration of the LLC

Registration is carried out through the electronic system of the state authorities of the Kyrgyz Republic.

  • Certificate of state registration;
  • Extract on the registered legal entity.

6. Tax Registration

After registration, the LLC is registered with the tax authorities.

  • Taxpayer registration card.

7. Company Seal Production

A corporate LLC seal is produced.

8. Opening a Bank Account

The LLC director must personally be present when opening a bank account.

Main Documents for the Bank

  • Director‚Äôs passport with notarized translation;
  • LLC Charter;
  • Founder‚Äôs decision;
  • Order appointing the director;
  • Certificate of registration and extract;
  • Taxpayer registration card;
  • Specimen signature and seal card;
  • LLC seal;
  • Power of attorney.

9. Licensing (If Required)

For licensed types of activities, we assist in obtaining licenses from the authorized state authorities.

10. Timeframes

  • LLC registration ‚Äî 3‚Äì5 working days;
  • Tax registration ‚Äî 1‚Äì2 working days;
  • Bank account opening ‚Äî 1‚Äì3 working days;
  • Licensing ‚Äî up to 30 working days.

11. Result for the Client

  • registered LLC;
  • tax registration;
  • corporate seal;
  • bank account;
  • licenses (if required);
  • full package of incorporation documents.

12. Additional Provisions

  • 100% foreign ownership is permitted;
  • no minimum charter capital is established;
  • remote registration via power of attorney is possible;
  • personal presence is required only at the bank.
